Who we are

We are the Youth Union of Tan Hung Ward, Ho Chi Minh city. Our members lead practical activities that benefit residents of all ages and serve as a bridge between the ward and the people.

What we focus on

  • Youth Development: Organizing training programs, competitions, and skill-building activities for young people.
  • Education & Technology: upporting learning, digital literacy, and innovative applications of technology.
  • Community Engagement: Connecting youth with local authorities to participate in public service, social security, and cultural preservation.
  • Volunteerism: Leading campaigns and projects in environmental protection, social support, and charity work.
